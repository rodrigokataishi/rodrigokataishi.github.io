Who Am I

I'm a researcher and team manager.
I have a Ph.D. from the Vilfredo Pareto Doctorate in Economics
(University of Turin and Real Collegio Carlo Alberto, Italy)

The topics of my work are related to Innovation, Technological Change and Firm's Capacities in developing Countries, in particular Latin America. My doctoral thesis concerns Technological change, productivity and macroeconomics.

These are my current positions:

Tierra del Fuego National University (Argentina)

I am an Associate Professor in Economics.

Turin University (Italy)

I am a Post-Doc Researcher (Ricercatore Afferente) on Biased Technological Change topics.
